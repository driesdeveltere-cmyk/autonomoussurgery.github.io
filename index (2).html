A surgeon's perspective

What would it actually take?

As someone who operates daily, here is what full surgical autonomy really requires.

🧠

Situational Awareness

Surgeons interpret subtle tissue tension, unexpected bleeding, and anatomical variation in real time. Current AI can classify images — it cannot yet read an operating field the way a trained surgeon does.

⚖️

Regulatory Approval

FDA and CE clearance for an autonomous surgical device would require years of clinical trials, post-market surveillance, and a liability framework that does not yet exist. Three years is ambitious for paperwork alone.

📡

The Research Horizon

Robotic-assisted surgery (da Vinci, etc.) is remarkable — but it amplifies the surgeon, it does not replace them. Fully autonomous closed-loop surgery remains an open research problem.
